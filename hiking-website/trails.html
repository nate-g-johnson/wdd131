Leave No Trace

As you enjoy the outdoors, please help preserve it for others by following the 7 Leave No Trace principles:

  1. Plan ahead and prepare
  2. Travel and camp on durable surfaces
  3. Dispose of waste properly
  4. Leave what you find
  5. Minimize campfire impact
  6. Respect wildlife
  7. Be considerate of others

By practicing Leave No Trace, you help ensure our trails stay beautiful and accessible for future generations.
