Hike Local - Site Plan

Site Name

Hike Local – This name reflects the site’s focus on exploring local hiking trails and outdoor activities in your region. It's memorable, clear, and fits the outdoorsy brand feel.

Optional domain availability: hikewithlocals.com (check availability if launching live)

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide an informative and engaging resource for hiking enthusiasts. It will include trail guides, tips for new hikers, gear recommendations, and community-submitted trail reviews. The site encourages people to explore nature, stay healthy, and connect with others who enjoy the outdoors.

Scenarios

  • What are the best easy-to-moderate trails near my city for a weekend hike?
  • What essential gear should I bring for a day hike in different seasons?

Color Schema

The color palette will reflect natural, earthy tones for a clean and grounded outdoor feel:

  • Forest Green (#225023) – Used for headings, buttons, and accents.
  • Soft Beige (#F5F5DC) – Used for background elements and card containers.

Typography

  • Heading Font: Segoe UI – Used for all titles and headings to create a modern, clean look.
  • Body Font: Verdana – Used for general paragraph and list text for readability.
